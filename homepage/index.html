Xinyu Vanessa Kwok, is a talented and dynamic Graphic Designer originally from Hong Kong who is currently pursuing her bachelor studies in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design in New York. With a passion for visual storytelling and a keen eye for design aesthetics, Xinyu enjoys exploring unique cultural perspectives to her creative work, such as blending the rich artistic influences of Hong Kong with the diverse and dynamic design landscape of New York. Her portfolio includes a range of projects, from branding and print design to digital media campaigns. Xinyu's work is characterized by its fusion of traditional Asian elements with modern design principles, reflecting her commitment to bridging cultural influences through her art.
